About

-Documentation currently unfinished-
To start, input your target's latitude and longitude with the "Set Destination button" and give it a name.
You can go into the menu in the top right from here and set the current destination as either a default location or a saved location in your favorites list for future use.
The center of the screen shows a 16 point minimalistic compass wheel that will use your phones GPS system to show your set destination relative to your current position.
Above the main compass is a few pieces of information that display your current latitude and longitude as well as the distance between you and your destination.
The top right corner shows you the compass' azimuth, pointing towards magnetic north giving you an idea of what direction your facing at any given moment.
