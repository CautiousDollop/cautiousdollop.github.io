What is KroFly?

KroFly is a minimalist compass-style app that guides you toward your saved destinations using GPS and device sensors.

Does KroFly work offline?

Yes. Once a destination is saved, KroFly works fully offline using your device's compass and location sensors.

Is my location data stored?

No. KroFly only stores locations *you* save, locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server unless you export it yourself.

How can I support the app?

Use it, share it, and optionally watch an ad to help cover API costs. Thanks for keeping it free for everyone!
